About us

Our ultimate goal is friendship and growth (both personal and professional) that's why we are launching off-line clubs for all the geeks out there.


CONTENT

Local Geeklers decides what to learn about and who listen to. We believe that LIVE CODING - is the best tech content for Developers. Few HRs are always join Geekle clubs with SOFT SKILLS problems talk topics.

FUNDING

Crowdfunding helps us to stay independent and choose content we need, instead of listening to the sales speeches from sponsors. We never sell your attention, never make you to listen speaker because some company paid for that.

GLOBAL NETWORKING

Geekle Fees paid by attendees helps us to launch Geekle Clubs in new countries and cities. Today you can find us 4 places: Palo Alto CA, Berlin, Amsterdam, Kiev. We dream to explore up to 100 cities in 2020.

We believe that who knows you and who you are - defines your future.
